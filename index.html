About

BB
Finance Economics Kathmandu Capital Markets Impact Research
"Financial literacy is not a privilege — it's a right. My mission is to make complex economic ideas accessible to everyone."

I'm Brijesh Bhattarai, a Financial Analyst based in Kathmandu, Nepal, currently at Impact Hub Kathmandu — an innovation hub bridging entrepreneurship, social impact, and sustainable finance across the Nepali ecosystem.

My professional journey spans financial analysis, investment research, and economic policy. I've worked with Hathway Investment Nepal Limited and A. Stotz Investment Research, building deep expertise in equity markets and behavioral finance.

Nepal's capital market is at a pivotal moment. I'm passionate about contributing to its growth through research, advocacy, and education — helping individuals and institutions navigate an evolving financial landscape.
